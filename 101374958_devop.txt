Information about the COMP 3104 DevOps course.

The COMP 3104 course, typically referred to as DevOps,
 is part of the curriculum in various computer science and information technology programs at institutions like George Brown College.

 The course focuses on the integration of development (Dev) and operations (Ops) to improve the collaboration and productivity of software development teams. 
 It emphasizes practices, tools, and methodologies that support continuous integration, continuous delivery, and automated deployment.
